Motivation
& Idea
Under the pression of increasing fluctuations in the demand, an agile and structurally flexible Supply Chain is to be developed.
Today's challenges and trends meet promising technologies to enable new solutions.
A platform is being develop to enable an agile and structurally flexible Supply Chain. It connects suppliers and demanders of short-term capacities in production, assembly and transportation.
Suppliers register their free capacities, prices and capabilities on the platform. Demanders indicate their requirements in term of product, quantity and deadline. The platform then creates optimal matches and supports the rapid development of an "on-demand Supply Chain".
